Kyiv Chicken

About the Dish

Kyiv chicken is a classic Ukrainian dish consisting of tender chicken breast filled with garlic butter, breaded and fried until golden. It is crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts – 500 g 18 oz
  • Butter – 120 g 4.2 oz
  • 2 eggs
  • Flour – 60 g 2.1 oz
  • Cooking oil – for frying
  • Breadcrumbs
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh parsley

Cooking Process

  1. Mix butter, garlic, and parsley. Shape into a log and freeze.
  2. Pound chicken breasts flat and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Place butter in the center and tightly roll the chicken.
  4. Chill for 30 minutes.
  5. Coat in flour, eggs, and breadcrumbs.
  6. Fry at 180°C 356°F until golden.
  7. Bake for 10 minutes to finish cooking.
